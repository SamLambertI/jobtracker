# Job Tracker — User Manual

A simple guide for your team to get started with Job Tracker.

---

## Getting Started

### Signing Up (Company Owner)

1. Go to the app and click **Create an account**
2. Enter your name, company name, email, and password
3. Check your email and click the confirmation link
4. You're in — you're the **Owner** of your company account

### Joining via Invite (Team Members)

1. Your manager will send you an invite link (by email or message)
2. Click the link — it opens the **Accept Invite** page
3. Enter your name and set a password
4. Check your email for a confirmation link, click it, then sign in

### Forgotten Your Password?

1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email — you'll receive a reset link
3. Click the link and set a new password

Everyone in the company has one of four roles. What you can see and do depends on your role:

| | Owner | Manager | Team Leader | Operative |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| View dashboard & jobs | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Create & edit jobs | Yes | Yes | — | — |
| Delete jobs | Yes | — | — | — |
| View quoted costs (£) | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| View actual costs (£) | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Log actual costs | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Edit quoted costs | Yes | Yes | — | — |
| Upload photos & notes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Use the calendar | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Reschedule jobs | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Manage teams | Yes | Yes | — | — |
| Invite & remove members | Yes | — | — | — |
| Manage price list | Yes | Yes | — | — |
| Post daily updates | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |

**Operatives** can view jobs, upload site photos, add timeline notes, clock in/out, and post daily updates — but they can't see any financial information (no costs, quotes, or margins).

## The Dashboard

The home screen gives you a snapshot of how things are going:

- **Active Jobs** — how many jobs are live (not yet invoiced)
- **Total Quoted** — the total value of all active quotes *(not visible to operatives)*
- **Total Actual** — what's been spent so far *(not visible to operatives)*
- **Profit / Margin** — are you making or losing money overall *(not visible to operatives)*

### Profitability Traffic Lights

Throughout the app you'll see coloured dots next to jobs:

- **Green** — On or under budget (10%+ margin). All good.
- **Amber** — Within 10% of budget. Keep an eye on it.
- **Red** — Over budget. Costs have exceeded the quote.
- **Grey** — No actual costs logged yet.

*(Traffic lights are not visible to operatives.)*

### Jobs Needing Attention

Below the stats, you'll see any jobs that are over budget or getting close. Tap one to see the detail.

## Jobs

### Viewing Jobs

Go to **Jobs** from the top menu (or bottom tab on mobile). You'll see all your company's jobs. Use the filter buttons at the top to show only jobs with a certain status or assigned to a specific team.

### Creating a Job (Owner / Manager)

1. Tap **New job**
2. Fill in the client name (required) and any other details — address, email, phone, description
3. Assign a team if you want
4. Set start and end dates if known
5. Tap **Create job**

### Job Statuses

Jobs move through these stages:

**Quoted** → **Booked** → **In Progress** → **Complete** → **Invoiced**

To change a job's status, open the job and tap the status button (e.g. "Mark as Booked"). You can only move forward one step at a time, or back one step if needed.

### Job Detail Page

Tap any job to see its full detail:

- **Client info** — address, email, phone
- **Financial summary** — quoted total, actual total, profit *(not visible to operatives)*
- **Cost breakdown** — a table comparing quoted vs actual costs by category *(not visible to operatives)*
- **Quick actions** — links to timeline, photos, quoted costs, actual costs
- **Clock in/out** — clock in to this job directly from the detail page

## Clock In / Out

The clock feature tracks when you arrive at and leave site.

### How to Clock In

1. Tap **Clock** from the bottom tab bar (mobile) or top menu (desktop)
2. Optionally select a job from the dropdown — or leave it as "No job" for yard work or general tasks
3. Tap the big green **Clock in** button
4. Your phone will ask for location permission — allow it to drop a GPS pin

You can also clock in directly from a **job detail page** — this automatically assigns you to that job.

### While Clocked In

- You'll see a green pulsing dot and your elapsed time
- You can change which job you're allocated to at any time using the dropdown
- Tap the red **Clock out** button when you leave site — another GPS pin is captured

### GPS Location

When you clock in and out, the app captures your GPS location (with your permission). Managers can see these locations as map pins on the timesheet to verify you were on site.

## Timesheet

The timesheet shows a weekly breakdown of hours.

### Viewing Your Timesheet

1. Go to **More** → **Timesheet** (or **Clock** page → **View timesheet**)
2. You'll see your hours grouped by day, showing which job you were on, clock in/out times, and duration
3. Use the **Prev** / **Next** buttons to view previous weeks

### Viewing Team Timesheets (Owner / Manager)

Owners and managers can see everyone's timesheets:

1. Go to the **Timesheet** page
2. Use the name filter buttons at the top to view a specific team member, or tap **Everyone** to see all
3. Each entry shows clock times, duration, job name, and clickable GPS map pins ("In pin" / "Out pin")

### Entries Without a Job

If someone clocked in without selecting a job (e.g. yard work), it shows as *"No job allocated"* on the timesheet.

## Logging Costs

### Quoted Costs (Owner / Manager)

These are what you've quoted the client. Open a job and tap **Quoted costs**.

1. Choose a category (Labour, Materials, Plant Hire, Waste, Other)
2. Enter a description, quantity, unit, and unit cost
3. The line total calculates automatically
4. Tap **Add line**

**Tip:** If you've set up a **Price List**, use the dropdown at the top to auto-fill from your standard rates. You can still change any value before saving.

### Actual Costs (Owner / Manager / Team Leader)

These are what you're actually spending. Open a job and tap **Actual costs**.

1. Same fields as quoted costs — category, description, quantity, unit, unit cost
2. You can also upload a **receipt photo** from your phone camera
3. Tap **Log cost**

The app automatically compares actual vs quoted costs to show your profitability.

## Price List

*(Owner / Manager only)*

Set up standard prices so your team doesn't have to remember rates.

1. Go to **Price List** from the menu
2. Add items with a category, name, unit, and unit cost
3. These items then appear as a dropdown when logging quoted or actual costs on any job

For example: "Type 1 Sub-Base — £40.00/tonne". When logging costs, select it from the dropdown and it fills in automatically. You can override the price on any individual job.

## Calendar

The calendar shows all scheduled jobs visually.

- **Month view** — see the full month at a glance
- **Week view** — more detail across 7 days (desktop only)
- **Day view** — one day at a time (default on mobile)

### Rescheduling

Drag a job to a new date to reschedule it. If other jobs depend on the same team, the app will ask if you want to cascade the date change to those jobs too.

### Mobile

On your phone, swipe left/right to move between days. The calendar defaults to day view on mobile for easier reading.

---

## Photos & Timeline

### Uploading Photos

Open a job → tap **Photos** → choose a photo from your camera or gallery. Add an optional caption. Photos are stored against the job with your name and the date/time.

### Timeline

Open a job → tap **Timeline** to see all activity on that job:

- Notes added by team members
- Status changes (e.g. "Status changed to In Progress")
- Costs logged
- Photos uploaded

Anyone can add a text note to the timeline — useful for leaving updates about what happened on site.

---

## Daily Updates

Every team member can post an end-of-day update.

1. Go to **Updates** from the menu (or via **More** on mobile)
2. Write what you got done today (required)
3. Optionally write what's coming up next
4. Tap **Post update**

Your managers and the company owner will be notified by email when you post an update.

The feed shows all updates from the last 7 days, grouped by date, so managers can see what everyone's been up to.

---

## Teams & Members

### Managing Teams (Owner / Manager)

Go to **Teams** to create and manage your teams.

1. Tap **New team** and give it a name
2. Edit a team to assign a **team leader**
3. Teams are used to organise who works on which jobs

### Managing Members (Owner only)

Go to **Members** to see everyone in your company.

- **Invite someone** — enter their email, choose a role, and optionally assign them to a team. They'll get an email with a link to join. You can also copy the invite link and send it yourself.
- **Change team** — use the dropdown next to each member to move them between teams
- **Remove** — remove someone from your company

## Mobile Navigation

The app is designed to work well on your phone:

- **Bottom tab bar** — five tabs for quick access:
  - **Home** — dashboard overview
  - **Calendar** — job schedule
  - **Jobs** — full job list
  - **Clock** — clock in/out
  - **More** — updates, timesheet, settings, and management pages
- **Large tap targets** — buttons and inputs are sized for thumbs
- **Card layouts** — tables become stacked cards on small screens
- **Camera access** — upload receipt photos and site photos directly from your phone camera
- **Swipe navigation** — swipe left/right on the calendar day view
